**What policy interventions work?** A systematic review and meta-analysis of the literature identifies evidence-based options that have achieved measurable improvements in high-quality experimental evaluations.

**What policy interventions make economic sense?** Using panel surveys, administrative data and budget papers, the benefits and costs associated with each policy option are calculated.

**What level of risk surrounds these estimates?** Estimates and assumptions are subjected to a risk assessment using Monte Carlo simulation to calculate the return on investment for each intervention.

**How to develop a portfolio of policy options?** Portfolio analysis determines which combination of policy interventions provides the best return on investment for a given budget.
